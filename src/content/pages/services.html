Vagal Nerve Regulation

Non-Invasive Vagus Nerve Simulation

The concept of passive or active electrically stimulating the auricular branch of the vagus nerve (ABVN) is now gaining recognition in literature. Called - non-invasive vagus nerve stimulation (VNS) - VNS has been reported to improve HRV, activate the parasympathetic system, to reduce inflammation, respiratory dysfunction, and the reduction of pro-inflammatory cytokines.

Dolphin Vagus Nerve Stimulation activates Vagal Tone

Dolphin Vagal Nerve Stimulation has been proven in science to activate the parasympathetic system (Armstrong 2019), which reduces lung inflammation (Kaniusas 2019), and pro-inflammatory cytokines to improve respiratory dysfunction (Tracey, 2007).

Scar Tissue Treatment

Scar tissue—whether from surgery, injury, or chronic inflammation—creates more than cosmetic concerns; it can disrupt fascial connections throughout your body, creating pain and dysfunction far from the original site. Our comprehensive scar tissue treatment protocols use specialized manual techniques to release adhesions, improve tissue mobility, and restore proper communication between affected areas.

By addressing these restrictions, we help eliminate pain patterns that may have persisted despite other interventions. Our integrated approach combines hands-on therapy with movement education and home care strategies, creating lasting improvements in both function and comfort while reducing reliance on pain medications.
